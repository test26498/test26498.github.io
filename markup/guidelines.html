WCAG 2.0 Guidelines

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines are categorized into four different principles: perceivable, operable, understandable and robust. Guidelines are also categorized into levels of conformance: A (lowest), AA, and AAA (highest). At this time, we strive to meet level A and AA guidelines. We will only go over level A and AA categorized guidelines here but you can find out more information about all the guidelines from the W3 Site.
